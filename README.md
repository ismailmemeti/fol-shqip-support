# fol-shqip-support
**Fol Shqip** is an iOS app that helps children learn Albanian through interactive games and activities.                                         
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Contact                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                   
  For support inquiries, please email: ismail.memeti@protonmail.ch                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                   
  Or [open an issue](https://github.com/ismailmemeti/fol-shqip-support/issues) on this repository.                                                 
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## FAQ                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                   
  **Q: What ages is this app for?**                                                                                                                
  A: Fol Shqip is designed for children ages 3-8.                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                   
  **Q: Does the app work offline?**                                                                                                                
  A: Yes, after downloading, the app works without an internet connection.                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                   
  **Q: How do I restore my purchases?**                                                                                                            
  A: Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases."                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                   
  **Q: How do I reset my child's progress?**                                                                                                       
  A: Go to Settings and tap "Reset Progress."                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Privacy                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                   
  Fol Shqip does not collect any personal data. All progress is stored locally on your device.
