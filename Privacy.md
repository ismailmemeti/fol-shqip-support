# Privacy Policy for Fol Shqip                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                   
  **Last updated: January 28, 2026**                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Overview                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                   
  Fol Shqip ("the App") is a children's language learning application designed to teach Albanian vocabulary. We take privacy seriously, especially 
  for our young users.                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Data Collection                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                   
  **We do not collect any personal data.**                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                   
  The App does not collect, store, or transmit:                                                                                                    
  - Personal information (names, emails, addresses)                                                                                                
  - Location data                                                                                                                                  
  - Device identifiers                                                                                                                             
  - Usage analytics                                                                                                                                
  - Photos or media                                                                                                                                
  - Contacts                                                                                                                                       
  - Advertising identifiers                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Local Data Storage                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                   
  All app data is stored **locally on your device only**, including:                                                                               
  - Learning progress and statistics                                                                                                               
  - Settings preferences                                                                                                                           
  - Child's display name (optional, entered by parent)                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                   
  This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.                                                             
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Children's Privacy                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                   
  Fol Shqip is designed for children and complies with:                                                                                            
  - Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)                                                                                               
  - Apple's App Store Guidelines for Kids                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                   
  We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Third-Party Services                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                   
  The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.                                                    
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## In-App Purchases                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                   
  In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to any payment or billing information.                                   
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Internet Connection                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                   
  The App functions fully offline. No internet connection is required after the initial download.                                                  
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Data Sharing                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                   
  We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Changes to This Policy                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                   
  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
